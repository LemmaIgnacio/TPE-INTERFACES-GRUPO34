    BLOCKA is a visual puzzle game where each level presents an image broken into four parts. Each fragment has been rotated, and your goal is to bring them back to their original orientation.

    🕹️How to Play

    "Your goal is to reconstruct an image by rotating its four parts until they are all correctly aligned."

    Step 1: Start the Level

    • Click the "PLAY" button.
    • The timer begins, and the puzzle (called a “blocka”) appears on screen.

    Step 1: Start the Level

    • Left-click on a piece to rotate it counterclockwise (↺).
    • Right-click on a piece to rotate it clockwise (↻).
    • Each click rotates the piece by 90°.

      • 💡Tip: Look closely at edges and colors to figure out the correct orientation

    The challenge increases with every level: images appear scrambled and covered by visual filters that distort colors and shapes.

    As you progress, filters become more complex—and sometimes, each subimage has its own unique filter.

    A timer starts when you hit "PLAY" and stops once you complete the blocka, recording your best time.

    After that, you can return to the main menu or move on to the next level.

    When you finally solve the puzzle, all filters fade away, revealing the original image in full color.
